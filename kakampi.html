USER MANUAL - How to use the system

1. Getting Started (Login / Register)

On launch you see authentication panel. Use demo accounts or register new.

  • Resident: resident / fire123
  • Responder: kakampi / lipafire
  • BFP Personnel: bfp.lipa / rescue143

Or click REGISTER to create your own profile (Barangay, Role, Contact). After registration, login again.

2. Fire Emergency SOS Button

Press the big central "KAKAMPI SOS · FIRE EMERGENCY" red button to trigger an immediate fire alert.

  • The system logs incident, sends dispatcher notification, vibrates (if supported), shows visual pulse ring and dispatches responders. Incident appears in log.

3. Emergency Hotlines

Tap any hotline card (BFP Lipa, CDRMMO, Police) to simulate a call. It logs the action and shows a prompt with contact details. Helpful to quickly reach official numbers.

4. Incident Log & Database

All SOS alerts, hotline calls, login sessions and dispatches are stored in localStorage (browser). You can Clear Log button to wipe incident history. The dashboard shows total incidents and total registered users in realtime.

5. User Role & Information

After login, your badge shows role (Resident/Responder/BFP Personnel) and barangay. The live panel shows real time clock and fire risk indicator (HIGH between 10am–3pm, LOW at night).

6. Logout & Multi-Account

Click Logout at top right to return to login screen. Multiple users can register and each action identifies the user’s role and barangay in incident records.

7. Tips & Notifications

  • Browser may ask permission for desktop notifications - allow for real-time alert popups.
  • All data persists even after page refresh (demo database).
  • Use SOS only for actual emergencies practice; system logs timestamp with details.
